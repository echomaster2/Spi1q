# Privacy Policy for SPI Master

**Last Updated: March 31, 2026**

SPI Master ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
When you use SPI Master, we may collect:
- **Profile Information:** Name, birth date, and study goals provided in the settings.
- **Authentication Data:** If you use third-party login (like Google), we receive your basic profile info (email, name).

### 1.2 Usage Data
We collect data on how you interact with the app, including:
- Study progress and lesson completion.
- Quiz scores and performance metrics.
- Time spent on various modules.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
- Personalize your study plan via our AI Tutor.
- Track your progress and award achievements.
- Improve the app's curriculum and user experience.
- Provide customer support.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage:** Some data (like theme preferences) is stored locally on your device.
- **Cloud Storage:** Your progress is synced to our secure servers using 256-bit encryption.
- **Third Parties:** We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access the data we have about you.
- Request the deletion of your account and all associated data (available in the app settings).
- Opt-out of data collection (though this may limit app functionality).
